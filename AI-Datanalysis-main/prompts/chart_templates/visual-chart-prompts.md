# Visual Chart Prompts

## Muc dich

File nay mo ta nhom template sinh bieu do truc quan. Cac file trong nhom nay co cau truc gan giong nhau va khac nhau chu yeu o rang buoc ve truc, marker va cach hien thi.

## Nhom line, area va scatter

- `line_plot.txt`: huong dan ve line chart, series theo truc thoi gian hoac thu tu
- `area_plot.txt`: huong dan area chart va quy tac fill du lieu
- `scatter_2d_plot.txt`: huong dan scatter 2D de phan tich tuong quan
- `bubble_plot.txt`: mo rong scatter voi kich thuoc marker

## Nhom phan pho va so sanh

- `bar_plot.txt`: bieu do cot cho tong hop va so sanh nhom
- `histogram_plot.txt`: phan bo tan suat
- `box_plot.txt`: phan tan va outlier
- `violin_plot.txt`: phan bo du lieu voi mat do
- `heatmap.txt`: ban do nhiet cho ma tran hoac correlation
- `density_contour_plot.txt`: contour mat do

## Nhom cau truc cap va thanh phan

- `pie_plot.txt`: ty trong theo thanh phan
- `sunburst_plot.txt`: cap cha con dang vong tron
- `treemap_plot.txt`: cap cha con dang khoi
- `polar_plot.txt`: du lieu theo he truc cuc

## Nhom tai chinh va chuyen biet

- `candle_plot.txt`: candlestick cho du lieu mo cua cao thap

## Luu y

- cac template nay duoc chon thong qua router + prompt builder
- khi sua mot file, can dam bao ten cot dau vao va output Plotly van phu hop voi executor
